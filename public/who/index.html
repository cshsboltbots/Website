Who Are We?
We are 2344A. A Vex VRC team operating out of Clear Springs High School in League City, Texas.
Our History
Our team has history going all the way back to 2013. We have always operated at Clear Springs High School in League City, Texas. Our school is located in the Bay Area and as such has roots with Robonauts, and deep roots with the NASA robotics community.
Our Team
Our team is made up of a diverse skillset and many team members are also involved in clubs and sports outside and within our school community. We look forward to the upcomming compeititon season where hopefully we can make it to Worlds again this year. And we wish luck upon other teams and other teams especially in CCISD.
